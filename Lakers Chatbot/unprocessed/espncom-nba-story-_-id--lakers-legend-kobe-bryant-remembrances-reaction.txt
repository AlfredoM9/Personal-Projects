Lakers legend Kobe Bryant - Remembrances and reaction
<p>A look at the legacy of Lakers great Kobe Bryant, who was killed in a helicopter crash at the age of 41. (4:36)</p>
<p><a href="/nba/team/_/name/lal/los-angeles-lakers">Los Angeles Lakers</a> legend <a href="https://www.espn.com/nba/player/_/id/110/kobe-bryant">Kobe Bryant</a> died Jan. 26 at age 41. Bryant and his 13-year-old daughter, Gianna, were <a href="/nba/story/_/id/28569438/sources-kobe-bryant-daughter-gianna-die-helicopter-crash">among nine people killed in a helicopter crash in Calabasas, California</a>.</p>
<p>Bryant entered the NBA straight out of high school in 1996 and won five championships and one regular-season MVP award. <a href="/nba/story/_/id/28569415/the-nba-world-mourns-death-kobe-bryant">Across the world, players, coaches and fans mourned Bryant's death</a>. The NBA announced in February that the All-Star Game MVP award, which he won four times, would be renamed in his honor.</p>
<p>Sunday would have been Bryant's 42nd birthday. In honor of his memory, Nike <a href="/nba/story/_/id/29649655/nike-launching-mamba-week-honor-kobe-bryant">is launching "Mamba Week,"</a> celebrating his impact on the NBA and the sports world as a whole. On Monday, Aug. 24 -- 8/24 -- the NBA world will celebrate both of the jersey numbers worn during Bryant's 20-year playing career.</p>
