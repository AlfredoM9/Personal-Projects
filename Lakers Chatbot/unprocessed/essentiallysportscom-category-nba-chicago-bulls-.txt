<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Chicago Bulls basketball team is owned by Jerry Reinsdorf and plays in the Eastern Conference of the NBA. They were founded in 1966 and their base is at the United Center. The Chicago Bulls share this stadium with the NHL side Chicago Blackhawks. </span></p>
<p><img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-386125 aligncenter" height="1024" src="https://image-cdn.essentiallysports.com/wp-content/uploads/20200922123940/thumb-1920-596152.png" width="1365"/></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The franchise attained superstardom in the 90s, where they won six NBA titles. There were two three-peats when <a data-wpel-link="internal" href="http://www.essentiallysports.com/tag/michael-jordan/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Michael Jordan</a> was part of the team in this decade. </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Chicago Bulls and Detroit Pistons had a memorable rivalry in the late 80s and early 90s. The teams faced each other in three consecutive conference finals between 1989-1991. </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">On each occasion, the winner of this clash claimed the <a data-wpel-link="external" href="https://www.nba.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer external" target="_blank">NBA</a> title. </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">In 1992, the Chicago Bulls set a franchise record of 67 wins. Three years later, in Michael Jordan’s first full season after the short-lived baseball career, the Chicago Bulls became the first team to record 70 regular-season wins. That year they also became the first team to have 3 players on the NBA All-Defensive First Team. </span></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">After Jordan’s second retirement, the Chicago Bulls struggled and did not feature in the playoffs until 2005. After another lean year, they made the playoffs on 8 consecutive occasions from 2007 to 2015. </span></p>
